04 · WHAT IS DCP05 / 16
Distributed Compute Platform

A marketplace for Saudi GPUs,
with an API on top.

i.
Renters
Call an OpenAI-compatible API. Get ALLaM, JAIS, Falcon, SDXL, embeddings. Pay in SAR or USDC. Data never leaves the Kingdom.
ii.
Providers
Plug an M-series Mac, an RTX rig, or a rack of H100s into the network. Pass KYB. Get paid weekly by IBAN, 75% of revenue.
iii.
DCP
Routes every request to the right GPU, in the right region, under the right policy. Clears billing. Enforces PDPL. Takes 25%.

06 · HOW IT WORKS07 / 16
How it works

Call an API. We do the
ugly middle for you.

01
A request lands at api.dcp.sa — OpenAI-compatible, Arabic or English.
02
The router classifies it — PDPL-sensitive? content-policy clean? — and picks a region, model, provider, slot.
03
The GPU runs the job. Tokens stream back with sub-40 ms p95.
04
Billing clears in SAR. Providers see their share accrue in real time; payout every two weeks.
05
Every action logged, signed, held for seven years — PDPL & SAMA.
