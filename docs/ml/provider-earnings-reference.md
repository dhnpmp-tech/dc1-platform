## FAQ

**Q: Why does payback period increase from 8 months (70%) to 11 months (50%)?**
A: Lower utilization means fewer hours generating revenue, so it takes longer to recoup the initial GPU purchase cost.

**Q: What if electricity costs are higher than Saudi rates?**
A: The calculator uses Saudi industrial rates (~$0.05/kWh). In other regions (EU ~$0.18/kWh), electricity costs are 3-4x higher, making profitability much tighter. This is DCP's structural advantage.

**Q: Can I get higher utilization than 90%?**
A: Theoretically yes, but 90% is the realistic ceiling for continuous operation (need 10% downtime for maintenance, OS updates, thermal headroom). Most providers stabilize at 70-80%.

**Q: What about depreciation?**
A: These calculations show gross provider income. Providers should account for GPU depreciation (~15-20% year 1) when calculating true net profit.

**Q: Is H100 or H200 worth the investment?**
A: Yes, if you can achieve 70%+ utilization. At that rate, monthly earnings exceed most salaries ($2-3K/month net). However, demand is concentrated (enterprises, research) so requires active sales/partnership effort.
