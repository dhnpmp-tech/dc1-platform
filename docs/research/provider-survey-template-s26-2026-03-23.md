## Survey Analytics Framework

### Quantitative Targets

| Metric | Target | Rationale |
|--------|--------|-----------|
| Responses | 100+ | Statistically significant sample |
| Interest (D1 ≥ 7) | 40%+ | Indicates warm lead |
| Current renters | 15-20% | Validate existing market adoption |
| Interest from non-renters | 30%+ | Expand addressable market |
| Preferred rate ≤ $0.30/hr | 60%+ | Validate floor pricing |

### Key Analysis Questions

1. **Segmentation:**
   - Interest by organization type (cafe vs. university vs. server farm)?
   - Interest by current rental status?
   - Interest by GPU tier?

2. **Barriers:**
   - What's the #1 blocker by segment?
   - Does trust differ by type?

3. **Pricing:**
   - Is our floor price ($0.267/hr for RTX 4090) acceptable?
   - Do cafes vs. universities have different rate expectations?

4. **Support:**
   - What's the minimal viable support?
   - Do new vs. experienced renters need different levels?

5. **Conversion:**
   - Of warm leads (D1 ≥ 7), how many convert to sign-up?
   - What's the typical time-to-conversion?
