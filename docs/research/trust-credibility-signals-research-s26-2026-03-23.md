## Research Methodology

### Phase 1: Expert Interviews (5-8 interviews)
**Goal:** Understand what trust matters for marketplace success

**Interviewees:**
- Founder/CEO (vision, competitive edge, credibility anchors)
- CSO/Security Lead (audit status, escrow transparency, compliance)
- Provider Ops (what providers ask about in onboarding)
- Sales/Early Customer (what closed early users needed to hear)

**Questions:**
- What's been the #1 trust objection we've heard?
- What proof point, if we could provide it, would remove 80% of hesitation?
- What are founders' credentials/background (team credibility)?
- When will security audit be complete?
- How transparent can we be about escrow mechanics?
- What Vision 2030 alignment can we highlight?

### Phase 2: Buyer Trust Assessment (Survey + Interviews)
**Goal:** Understand buyer risk perception and credibility requirements

**Sample:** 15-20 buyers (mix of current Vast.ai/RunPod users + prospects)

**Survey Topics:**
1. **Initial Impression:**
   - How do you feel about trying a new GPU marketplace?
   - What's your biggest concern?
   - Rate trust level (1-10) before knowing anything about DCP

2. **Credibility Anchors (Test each):**
   - *Founder credentials:* "Founded by [names], previously at [companies]"
   - *Funding:* "Backed by [investors], raised $[amount]"
   - *Security:* "Smart contract audited by [firm], escrow on Ethereum mainnet"
   - *Compliance:* "Licensed in Saudi Arabia, complies with Vision 2030"
   - *Early users:* "50+ early adopters, 99.7% uptime in beta"
   - *Energy story:* "First GCC-based GPU marketplace, renewable energy powered"

   For each: "Does this make you more/less likely to try? (1-5 scale)"

3. **Risk Perception:**
   - Most likely failure scenario: [open text]
   - How would you mitigate that risk?: [open text]
   - Would you do a trial first?: [yes/no/maybe]
   - How much should you test before going all-in?: [% of typical spend]

4. **Proof Points:**
   - Rank importance (1-10): Team credentials, Funding, Audit, Testimonials, Uptime SLA, Price guarantee
   - Which would you trust MOST from a new marketplace?: [ranking]

5. **Information Needs:**
   - What info would you want before sign-up?
   - What info would you want after sign-up?
   - How transparent should escrow/contract details be?

### Phase 3: Provider Trust Assessment (Survey + Interviews)
**Goal:** Understand provider risk perception for new platform

**Sample:** 15-20 providers (current active on Vast.ai/RunPod + cafe/farm operators)

**Survey Topics:**
1. **Initial Impression:**
   - How do you feel about adding a new marketplace to your portfolio?
   - What's your biggest concern about DCP specifically?
   - Rate trust level (1-10) for new platform

2. **Credibility Anchors (same as buyers, but provider-relevant):**
   - *Team credentials:* Do founders have GPU/data center experience?
   - *Funding:* Would VC backing increase confidence?
   - *Security:* Smart contract audit on escrow?
   - *Compliance:* Licensed in Saudi, Vision 2030 aligned?
   - *Early adopters:* How many active providers in beta?
   - *Energy story:* Interested in selling compute in energy-efficient context?

   For each: "Does this influence your likelihood to join? (1-5 scale)"

3. **Payment & Financial Risk:**
   - Biggest concern about getting paid?: [open text]
   - Preferred payment method?: [weekly/monthly/crypto/fiat]
   - Would escrow contract transparency help?: [yes/no/maybe]
   - What minimum weekly/monthly payment guarantee would you need?

4. **Operational Risk:**
   - Biggest concern about running daemon/managing jobs?: [open text]
   - What support would make you feel confident?
   - Would you want SLA (uptime guarantee) for platform availability?
   - Trial period preference (how long before going all-in)?

5. **Competitive Positioning:**
   - How does DCP compare to your current platform(s)?
   - What would make DCP your primary marketplace (vs secondary)?
   - Energy story interesting to you?: [1-10 scale]
   - Vision 2030 alignment important?: [yes/no/neutral]

## Key Trust Signals to Test

### For Buyers

| Signal | Messaging | Proof Point | Timeline |
|--------|-----------|-------------|----------|

## Potential Findings & Mitigation Strategies

### Likely Trust Barriers

**Barrier 1: "New Platform Risk"**
- Typical concern: "What if DCP disappears or gets hacked?"
- Mitigation: Smart contract audit, escrow transparency, founder track record
- Messaging: "Blockchain-backed escrow, audited contracts, no DCP control over payments"

**Barrier 2: "Geographic Risk"**
- Typical concern: "Saudi Arabia? Is that stable/legitimate?"
- Mitigation: Vision 2030 alignment, CCSEZ (government backing), regulatory compliance
- Messaging: "First GCC marketplace, backed by Saudi Vision 2030 energy & data center investment"

**Barrier 3: "Price Too Good to Be True"**
- Typical concern: "Why is DCP 24% cheaper? What's the catch?"
- Mitigation: Transparent energy cost advantage, competitor pricing education
- Messaging: "Saudi renewable energy costs $0.013/kWh vs EU $0.19/kWh — we pass savings through"

**Barrier 4: "Support Quality"**
- Typical concern: "Will support be available if something goes wrong?"
- Mitigation: Documented SLA, response time guarantees, live chat
- Messaging: "24/7 support, 1-hour response SLA, dedicated account reps for large users"

**Barrier 5: "Community Size"**
- Typical concern: "Are there enough users/jobs? Will it actually work?"
- Mitigation: Early adopter success stories, public metrics on activity
- Messaging: "[X] active providers, [Y] monthly transactions, [Z]% uptime"

## Regional/Cultural Considerations

**Saudi Context:**
- Vision 2030 is powerful credibility anchor (government backing)
- Renewable energy (solar) resonates with ESG-conscious users
- "First GCC marketplace" has competitive advantage appeal
- Trust in institutions/government is higher than in some Western markets
- Arabic language support may significantly increase trust (especially providers)

**International Buyers:**
- May not care about Saudi context as much
- Energy/sustainability story may resonate more
- Price advantage is primary motivator
- Need proof that platform actually works

**Providers (Local vs International):**
- Local providers: Care about Vision 2030, energy advantage, SAR payment
- International providers: Care about payment methods (USDC), uptime SLA
- Cafes/universities: Care about compliance, stability, support
