## Go/No-Go Decision Framework

### LAUNCH (Go Decision):
✓ All Tier 1 research shows >85% positive indicators
✓ No critical blockers blocking user onboarding
✓ Dashboard UX enables task completion
✓ Buyer/provider pricing perception acceptable
✓ Trust barriers have mitigation strategies

### CONDITIONAL LAUNCH (Go with Fixes):
✓ Critical blockers identified but fixable (<2 hours)
✓ Re-test top 2 blockers with 1-2 participants
✓ Engineering can fix and deploy same-day
✓ Risk is acceptable for live launch monitoring

### DELAY (No-Go Decision):
✗ >2 Tier 1 projects show <70% success
✗ Critical blockers are architecture-level (requires redesign)
✗ Multiple user segments can't complete core flows
✗ Trust barriers are unaddressable before launch

## Risk Mitigation & Contingencies

### If Recruitment is Slow:
- Expand target audience (team members, broader networks)
- Run with minimum viable sample (3-4 participants per research)
- Conduct unmoderated testing (less rich but faster)

### If Critical Blocker is Found:
- Escalate to engineering immediately with fix recommendation
- Most UX issues fixable within 1-2 hours
- Re-test with 1-2 participants post-fix
- May impact launch timing (communicate Monday 3/24)

### If Research Timeline Slips:
- Prioritize Tier 1 projects (report by 3/28)
- Defer Tier 2 projects to post-launch if needed (by 4/1)
- Daily status updates to engineering on research progress
