### 7.1 Renter Payments

**Billing method:**
- Pay-as-you-go: Only charged for jobs you submit
- Per-token metering: Charged based on actual compute time
- No monthly minimums or reserved capacity

**Charges include:**
- Model inference time (SAR per hour)
- Storage (if applicable)

- Currency conversion (if paying in USD)

**Invoices:**
- Issued monthly
- Available in your dashboard
- Include detailed job breakdown

### 7.2 Provider Payouts

**Earning Model:**
- You earn 75% of job revenue (25% infrastructure fee)
- Earnings calculated per job completed
- Transparent, real-time payout tracking

**Payment Method:**
- Direct transfer to your registered bank account
- Weekly or monthly withdrawals (your choice)
- Withdrawal limits: Min 50 SAR, Max 100,000 SAR/day
- Processing time: 1-2 business days

**Failed Payouts:**
- If your bank account is invalid, earnings are held in escrow
- We'll notify you within 7 days
- You can update your account within 30 days (after which we may donate unclaimed earnings)

### 7.3 Disputes & Refunds

- If a job fails due to platform error, full refund applies automatically

**Chargeback Protection:**
- If you request a payment chargeback, your account is immediately suspended

**Late Payments:**
- Accounts with 7+ days overdue balance will have job submissions suspended
