# Arabic Document Intelligence for Saudi Government Agencies

## One-Paragraph Summary

DC1 provides government agencies with PDPL-compliant, in-kingdom Arabic document processing at 50-60% below international cloud costs. Extract insights from policy documents, ministerial circulars, and regulatory archives without data leaving Saudi borders. Arabic-first NLP eliminates translation bottlenecks and preserves semantic accuracy in legal and regulatory language.

---

## Problem: Government Document Complexity at Scale

Saudi government agencies manage millions of documents—policy archives, ministerial circulars, regulatory guidance, compliance records—in both Arabic and English. Current workflows force agencies to:

- **Route sensitive data offshore** to international cloud providers (AWS, Azure, GCP), creating PDPL compliance risk
- **Rely on manual search** through unstructured archives, losing institutional knowledge and delaying policy analysis
- **Use translated-first tools** that lose nuance in Arabic regulatory language and ministerial directives
- **Budget 60% premium costs** for hyperscaler cloud services, straining IT budgets

---

## Solution: In-Kingdom, Arabic-First RAG

DC1 deploys Arabic Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) on local GPU infrastructure. Government agencies deploy private instances of our Arabic LLM + embedding + reranker stack, enabling:

1. **Policy Document Search**: Query across years of ministerial circulars and regulatory guidance in Arabic. Instantly surface precedents and related policies.
2. **Bilingual Query Interface**: Officials ask questions in Arabic; system returns insights from both Arabic and English document sets.
3. **Compliance Lookups**: Extract relevant regulations, compliance requirements, and deadlines from archives without data leaving in-kingdom.
4. **Institutional Knowledge Preservation**: Capture and search accumulated expertise across departments.
