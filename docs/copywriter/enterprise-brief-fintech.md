## Problem: Financial Compliance + Document Volume + Cost

Saudi financial institutions and fintech platforms face urgent bottlenecks:

- **Regulatory Compliance**: SAMA (Saudi Central Bank) mandates KYC/AML checks. Processing Arabic identity documents, financial declarations, and beneficial ownership forms is manual, slow, and error-prone.
- **Document Volume**: Banks process 100K+ financial forms monthly (mortgages, loans, investment accounts, regulatory filings). Manual review = hiring costs + processing delays.
- **Data Sovereignty**: International KYC platforms (Veriff, IDology, Thomson Reuters) route sensitive financial data and personal information offshore, creating PDPL exposure for any institution holding Saudi customer data.
- **Cost Pressure**: Hyperscaler ML services charge $1.50–$32/GPU-hour. Processing 100K documents monthly racks up $50K–$200K/month in cloud bills.
- **Arabic Accuracy**: Western KYC systems trained on English passports and Latin scripts. Arabic identity documents, VAT forms, and financial statements get lower accuracy.

---

## Solution: In-Kingdom Financial NLP

DC1 deploys private Arabic financial document processing directly on your infrastructure:

1. **KYC Document Extraction**: Automatically extract name, ID number, issuing date, beneficial ownership from Arabic national IDs, passports, and financial declarations. 95%+ accuracy.
2. **Financial Report Analysis**: Parse Arabic company financial statements (P&L, balance sheets), extract key ratios, flag anomalies, and generate compliance summaries.
3. **SAR/VAT Document Processing**: Process Arabic VAT returns, SAR-denominated invoices, and regulatory filings automatically.
4. **AML Risk Scoring**: Flag high-risk customer profiles, unusual transaction patterns, and politically exposed persons (PEPs) in Arabic-language documents.
5. **Loan & Investment Analysis**: Automatically review borrower financial documentation, calculate debt-to-income ratios, and approve/flag applications.
