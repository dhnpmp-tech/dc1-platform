## Problem: Confidentiality + Cost + Linguistic Accuracy

Saudi legal practitioners face a triangle of constraints:

- **Confidentiality Risk**: Sensitive client cases, contracts, and legal strategies uploaded to international platforms = regulatory and reputational exposure
- **Cost Barrier**: LexisNexis, Westlaw, and other Western legal research platforms charge $500–$2,000/month per user. No Arabic emphasis.
- **Linguistic Accuracy**: Legal terminology in Arabic (Islamic law, Sharia compliance, Saudi penal code) is non-trivial to translate. English-first models miss nuance.
- **Search Inefficiency**: Manual case law digging consumes 20–40% of lawyer billable time
