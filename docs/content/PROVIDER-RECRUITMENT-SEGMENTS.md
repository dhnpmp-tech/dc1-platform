## Segment 1: Internet Café Owners

### The Opportunity

**Problem They Have:**
- Gaming GPUs (RTX 4090, 4080) sit idle 16–20 hours/day
- Electricity is cheap in Saudi Arabia, but hardware ROI is slow
- Gaming revenue has plateaued; looking for new revenue streams

**DCP's Solution:**
- Turn idle GPUs into 24/7 income stream
- RTX 4090 earns **$145–$315/month net profit** (3–6 month payback)
- No gaming-specific expertise required; DCP handles job distribution
- Works alongside gaming (scheduled around player usage patterns)

### The Economics

**Example: Internet Café with 4x RTX 4090**

```
Setup: 4 GPUs × $400 each (current market) = $1,600 investment

Monthly Income (DCP):
- Per GPU/month: $180–$350 (70% utilization)
- 4 GPUs: $720–$1,400/month gross
- DCP fee (15%): –$108–$210
- Provider payout: $612–$1,190/month net

Electricity cost (Saudi rates: $0.048/kWh):
- 4 GPUs × 300W × 24h × 30 days = 86.4 MWh
- Cost: 86.4 × $0.048 = ~$4.15/month (negligible)

Net Monthly Profit: $600–$1,200/month
Payback Period: 1.3–2.7 months for hardware
Annual Profit: $7,200–$14,400/year on $1,600 investment (450–900% ROI)
```

- Want to diversify revenue beyond hosting contracts
- Looking for high-margin, technical-friendly markets

**DCP's Solution:**
- Monetize H100/H200/RTX GPU inventory on-demand
- H200 earns **$2,500–$4,500/month** ($2,320–$4,200 net profit)
- DCP handles customer acquisition, billing, support
- 85% revenue share (industry-leading for marketplace)
- Flexible capacity (commit only what you want)

## Email Campaign: 7-Email Sequence

### Email 1: Hook (Day 0)
**Subject:** "Your GPU Could Be Earning"
**Content:** Lead with segment-specific stat
- Café owner: "RTX 4090s earn $200–$350/month"
- University: "H100s fund research at $3K–$6K/month"
- Datacenter: "H200s generate $350K/year per rack"

### Email 2: Pain Point (Day 2)
**Subject:** "You're Leaving Money on the Table"
**Content:** How are they currently using (or not using) their GPU capacity?
- Idle gaming hours, research downtime, unutilized racks

### Email 3: Social Proof (Day 4)
**Subject:** "Meet Providers Already Earning"
**Content:** Case study from their segment (café owner, professor, datacenter manager)
- Quote, earnings, payback time

### Email 4: ROI Calculator (Day 6)
**Subject:** "What Could You Make?"
**Content:** Interactive (or linked) earnings calculator
- Input GPU model/count
- See monthly/annual projection
- Show payback timeline

### Email 5: FOMO + Offer (Day 8)
**Subject:** "Limited Spots: Free Trial + $100 Credit"
**Content:**
- We're recruiting top providers
- Free 2-week trial (no payment info needed)
- If they refer others, they get bonus

### Email 6: Objection Handling (Day 10)
**Subject:** "Common Questions: Security, Support, Payouts"
**Content:** FAQ addressing:
- Data privacy/security
- Customer support SLA
- Payment reliability
- Conflict with day-job (café gaming, research)

### Email 7: Close (Day 12)
**Subject:** "Ready to Start Earning?"
**Content:**
- Simple one-click onboarding
- FAQ + support contact
- "No pressure—let me know if you have questions"
