## Regional Economics Comparison

### Energy Cost Advantage by Region

| Region | Electricity Cost | vs Saudi | Competitive Advantage |
|--------|---|---|---|
| **Saudi Arabia** | $0.048-$0.053/kWh | Baseline | Lowest + vision 2030 push |
| **UAE** | $0.055-$0.065/kWh | +15% vs KSA | Lower than EU, high purchasing power |
| **Egypt** | $0.040-$0.050/kWh | -5% vs KSA | Cheapest, but smaller market |
| **Kuwait** | $0.060-$0.070/kWh | +20% vs KSA | Higher cost but wealthy market |
| **US Hyperscalers** | $0.08-$0.12/kWh | +70-100%+ | DCP is 50-70% cheaper |
| **EU Hyperscalers** | $0.18-$0.30/kWh | +260-500%+ | DCP is 70-80% cheaper |

**Bottom Line:** Even higher-cost MENA regions (UAE, Kuwait) undercut hyperscalers significantly. Electricity arbitrage is sustainable across MENA.
