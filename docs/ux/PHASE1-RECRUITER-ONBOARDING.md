## Recruitment Execution (Step-by-Step)

### LINKEDIN OUTREACH (Most Important - Fastest Results)

**For Each Persona:**

1. **Go to LinkedIn Search**
   - Click "Search people"
   - Enter search criteria from the checklist
   - Apply filters (location, title, seniority)
   - Save results

2. **Send Connection Requests**
   - Send personalized message (copy from checklist, customize with 1 personal detail)
   - Do NOT send generic "Let's connect" requests
   - Example: "Hi [Name], I noticed your work in [Company]'s legal tech platform. We're testing a new GPU marketplace for Arabic models and would love your perspective. Interested in 90 minutes for research participation? [Details]"

3. **Track & Follow-Up**
   - Log each connection request in your spreadsheet
   - Mark when they accept
   - Send message within 24 hours of acceptance
   - If no response after 3 days, consider them non-responsive

**Expected Conversion:**
- LinkedIn connection → message send: ~60% conversion
- Message → meeting interest: ~30-40% conversion
- Meeting interest → confirmed attendance: ~70-80% conversion
- **Overall:** Expect 1 confirmation per 7-10 connection requests

## Critical Success Factors

1. **Start immediately.** Every hour counts. Don't wait.
2. **Personalize messages.** Generic "Let's connect" requests get ~5% response rate. Personalized messages get 20-30%.
3. **Follow up persistently.** People get busy. 3-4 touches = norm.
4. **Track everything.** Spreadsheet is your friend. Update hourly.
5. **Confirm confirmations.** Get them to say "yes" twice (LinkedIn + email confirmation).
6. **Communicate clearly.** Confusion = cancellations. Over-communicate logistical details.
