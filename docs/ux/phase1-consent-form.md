# Phase 1 User Testing — Informed Consent Form

**Study Title:** DCP (Decentralized Compute Platform) Phase 1 Renter Journey Validation
**Researcher:** UX Research Team, DCP
**Study Date:** March 25-26, 2026
**Study Duration:** 90 minutes

---

## Purpose of Study

We are conducting user research to evaluate the usability and user experience of DCP, a new GPU compute marketplace with Arabic-native models. This study aims to identify strengths and areas for improvement in the renter journey (discovery, template evaluation, deployment, and job monitoring).

**What we're testing:**
- Can users discover Arabic LLM models quickly?
- Is pricing transparent and competitive?
- Is the deployment flow intuitive?
- Are users confident in the platform?

---

## What We Will Do

During this 90-minute session, you will:

1. **Warmup (10 min):** Discuss your background with GPU marketplaces and current pain points
2. **Cold-Start Discovery (15 min):** Search for an Arabic LLM template suited to your needs
3. **Template Evaluation (15 min):** Compare 3 template options and pricing
4. **Deploy Flow (20 min):** Configure and deploy a template (we'll stop before actual payment)
5. **Job Monitoring (10 min):** Review how to monitor a running job
6. **Sentiment & NPS (10 min):** Share your Net Promoter Score and likelihood to recommend
7. **Debrief (10 min):** Open feedback and discussion

**What you'll interact with:**
- Live DCP marketplace interface (api.dcp.sa)
- Sample templates and models
- Pricing comparison tool
- Deployment configuration form

## Data Use & Confidentiality

### How We'll Use Your Data
- **Analysis:** Identify patterns in user behavior, pain points, and success rates
- **Product Improvement:** Recommendations for UX fixes and feature prioritization
- **Reporting:** Aggregate findings in Phase 1 launch readiness report
- **Sharing:** May share anonymized quotes in team documentation and investor updates

### Confidentiality
- Your personal information (name, company, email) will be stored separately from session data
- Session recordings/transcripts will be labeled with a **Participant ID** (e.g., "P-A1"), not your name
- We will never publicly identify you without explicit permission
- Only core team members (5-10 people) will access raw session data

### Data Retention
- Session recordings: Kept for 6 months, then deleted
- Survey responses: Kept for 12 months for trend analysis
- Notes/analysis: Indefinitely (to inform product roadmap)
