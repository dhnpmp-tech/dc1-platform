## Testing Execution (3/25-3/26)

### Session Protocol: 90 Minutes (Per Participant)

**Part 1: Warmup** (10 min)
- Background and experience with GPU marketplaces
- Current pain points with existing platforms

**Part 2: Cold-Start Discovery** (15 min)
- **Task:** "Find a good option for deploying an Arabic LLM with budget awareness"
- **Metrics:** Time to discovery, model chosen, confidence level
- **Success:** <60 seconds to find Arabic model, >70% confidence in choice

**Part 3: Template Evaluation** (15 min)
- **Task:** "Compare the pricing and features of 3 templates; tell me which you'd choose"
- **Metrics:** Template comparison time, price perception clarity, trust in data
- **Success:** >80% can articulate price advantage, <3 minutes to decide

**Part 4: Deploy Flow** (20 min)
- **Task:** "Configure and deploy a template (stop before payment)"
- **Metrics:** Completion rate, friction points, confidence in setup
- **Success:** >70% complete unassisted, <5 critical blockers

**Part 5: Job Monitoring** (10 min)
- **Task:** "Show me how to monitor your running job and check performance"
- **Metrics:** Dashboard usability, understanding of cost/performance tradeoff
- **Success:** >80% can identify key metrics

**Part 6: Sentiment & NPS** (10 min)
- **Metric:** Net Promoter Score (0-10), likelihood to recommend
- **Target:** NPS ≥7, would recommend ≥80%

**Part 7: Debrief** (10 min)
- Open feedback, pain points, feature requests
- Clarification on blockers from earlier steps
