## Go/No-Go Decision Framework

### 🟢 GREEN LIGHT (GO) — Approve Phase 2

**All of the following must be true:**

- [ ] Total revenue ≥ $500 (enough to prove demand)
- [ ] ≥3 renters active (enough to diversify customer base)
- [ ] ≥5 providers active (supply availability proven)
- [ ] Provider margins ≥ $30/month (incentive to stay)
- [ ] CAC payback ≤ 2.5 months (sustainable acquisition)
- [ ] Repeat rate ≥ 15% (renter stickiness)
- [ ] No critical technical issues (system reliability)
- [ ] Provider churn < 10% (retention healthy)

**If GREEN:**
→ **ACTIVATE PHASE 2** immediately
→ Deploy $50-75K growth budget (DCP-745, DCP-746, DCP-747)
→ Accelerate provider recruitment and renter marketing
→ Target: Break-even by Month 3 of Phase 2 (June 2026)

---

### 🟡 CONDITIONAL GO — Approve Phase 2 with Caution

**If majority of criteria met but 1-2 areas below target:**

- [ ] Revenue $300-500 (good signal but lower than target)
- [ ] 2-3 renters active (small but viable)
- [ ] 3-5 providers active (minimal supply)
- [ ] Provider margins $20-50/month (below target but workable)
- [ ] CAC payback 2.5-3.5 months (acceptable but tight)
- [ ] Repeat rate 10-15% (okay but needs improvement)
- [ ] Minor technical issues (resolved, no ongoing impact)
- [ ] Provider churn 5-10% (watch closely)

**If CONDITIONAL GO:**
→ **APPROVE PHASE 2 WITH CONDITIONS**
→ Deploy $30-40K growth budget (cautious approach)
→ Focus on retention and utilization over acquisition
→ Monthly check-in with founder on metrics
→ Target: Break-even by Month 4-5 (July-August 2026)

---

### 🔴 RED LIGHT (NO-GO) — Defer or Pivot

**If 2+ critical criteria fail:**

- [ ] Revenue < $300 (insufficient market validation)
- [ ] <2 renters active (no sustainable customer base)
- [ ] <3 providers active (supply side broken)
- [ ] Provider margins < $20/month (providers won't stay)
- [ ] CAC payback > 3.5 months (acquisition economics fail)
- [ ] Repeat rate < 10% (renters not returning)
- [ ] Critical technical issues (system unreliable)
- [ ] Provider churn > 10% (rapid supply loss)

**If RED (NO-GO):**
→ **DEFER PHASE 2**
→ Enter Phase 1b (optimization mode)
→ Diagnose: Market, product, operations, or economics issue?
→ Fix root cause (2-3 weeks minimum)
→ Re-assess at 2026-04-15 for Phase 2 restart

## Red Flags That Override Other Metrics

**Any of these findings automatically trigger NO-GO recommendation:**

1. **Data integrity issue** — Revenue or cost data cannot be trusted (requires recount)
2. **Security incident** — User data exposed, payment system compromised, or significant hack
3. **Provider exodus** — >30% churn in single day (indicates fundamental issue)
4. **Regulatory blocker** — Legal action, compliance violation, or licensing issue
5. **Technical failure** — System down >2 hours, data loss, or recovery required
6. **Fraud detected** — Fake transactions, bot activity, or payment fraud

**If any red flag detected:** Immediately escalate to founder + legal/security
