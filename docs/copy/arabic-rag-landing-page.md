## Why PDPL Compliance Matters

Saudi Arabia's Personal Data Protection Law (2023, enforcement 2024) is the regional standard:

- **Scope:** Any organization processing data of Saudi residents
- **Consequence:** Data processed outside KSA = non-compliance
- **Penalty:** Up to 5% annual revenue or SAR 5M, whichever is greater
- **Enforcement:** PDPL Authority audits banking, government, healthcare, telecom

**DC1's Compliance Model:**
- All compute happens on Saudi infrastructure
- No data transit to foreign cloud providers
- Cryptographic verification of in-kingdom processing
- Audit logs for regulatory review
- Compatible with PDPL Authority technical standards
