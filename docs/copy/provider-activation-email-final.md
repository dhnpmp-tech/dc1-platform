## Bilingual Tone & Appropriateness Notes

### Saudi Market Sensitivities
1. **Respect for Infrastructure** — Emphasize how DC1 protects provider investments, not exploits them
2. **Community Benefit** — Highlight how provider earnings support families, businesses, and institutions
3. **Government Alignment** — Mention Saudi Vision 2030 and local tech investment
4. **Religious/Cultural Sensitivity** — Avoid colloquialisms; use professional Modern Standard Arabic
5. **Privacy** — Emphasize that DC1 is PDPL-compliant and data stays in-kingdom

### Tone Adjustments by Segment
- **Gaming Centre / Café Owners:** Friendly, practical, earnings-focused
- **University/Research:** Academic, mission-aligned, long-term partnership framing
- **Enterprise (H100 Owners):** Professional, ROI-focused, scalability messaging

### Bilingual Best Practices
- All monetary amounts shown in both USD and SAR (when appropriate)
- Key CTA buttons use English/Arabic toggle or separate links
- Reply email address is universal (providers@dcp.sa)
- No slang, colloquialisms, or informal speech in Arabic
