### Pillar 3: Native Arabic Linguistic Accuracy & Regional Specialization

**Headline:** "Trained on Arabic. Optimized for Arab Markets."

**Body Copy:**
Most commercial LLMs (GPT-4, Claude, Gemini) are trained primarily on English, with Arabic as an afterthought. They excel at translation but fail at native reasoning in Arabic.

DC1's Arabic RAG uses **ALLaM-7B** (Saudi-backed), **JAIS-13B** (TII/UAE), and **Falcon-H1** (TII), all trained natively on Arabic with code-switching and local dialect support. **BGE-M3 embeddings** understand Arabic semantic relationships. **BGE-Reranker** ranks Arabic search results by true relevance, not keyword matching.

The result: Your RAG system understands Modern Standard Arabic, Gulf dialects, legal terminology, financial jargon, medical concepts, and cultural context that English-first models completely miss.

**Real-world example:** A Saudi legal firm uploads 50,000 contract documents in Arabic. Bedrock's Claude misses nuance in classical Arabic phrases and Sharia-informed clauses. DC1's JAIS + BGE stack retrieves correct precedents, understands legal context, and generates accurate opinions in native Arabic syntax.
