## FAQ Section (Homepage)

### Question 1: "Is my data stored on DC1 servers? PDPL compliance?"

**English Answer:**

Yes, all data stays on Saudi Arabia GPUs located at our partner data centers in Riyadh. DC1 operates under the **Saudi Personal Data Protection Law (PDPL)**, which legally requires all personal/sensitive data to remain in-kingdom.

For Arabic models (ALLaM, Falcon, JAIS, RAG stack), this is mandatory — your embeddings, LLM prompts, and document indexes are never exported. You own all output. DC1 does not retain or train on customer data.

For compute jobs (PyTorch, Jupyter, custom containers), your code and intermediate outputs stay on your rented GPU. Logs are stored in-kingdom (your dashboard) for 30 days, then deleted.

**Arabic Answer:**

نعم، جميع البيانات تبقى على معالجات الرسوميات في المملكة العربية السعودية. DC1 يعمل تحت قانون حماية البيانات الشخصية (PDPL)، الذي يتطلب قانونياً بقاء جميع البيانات في المملكة.

جميع النماذج العربية (ALLaM, Falcon, JAIS, RAG) تعمل محلياً. لا تحتفظ DC1 ببيانات العميل أو تستخدمها للتدريب.

### Question 2: "How is pricing calculated? When do I pay?"

**English Answer:**

Pricing is **pay-per-use**, calculated in real time:

- **LLM inference:** $0.08-0.22 per million tokens (depends on model size)
- **Image generation:** $0.0025-0.003 per image
- **GPU hours:** $0.15-2.50/hour (depends on GPU type: RTX 4090, H100, H200, A100)
- **Embeddings:** $0.02 per million tokens

You're billed **hourly** for GPU rentals and **per-request** for API inference. No monthly fees, no minimums. Cancel anytime. Billing runs through your wallet (top-up in dashboard with credit card, bank transfer, or crypto). Invoice available 24/7.

Example: Run Llama 3 for 2 hours on RTX 4090 = 2 hrs × $0.22/hr = **$0.44 total**.
