### Visual Style Guidelines
- **Color palette:** Navy `#0D1B2A` backgrounds, teal `#00E5C8` accents, orange `#FF6B00` energy

- **Motion:** Smooth, data-flow aesthetic — think network nodes and inference pipelines
- **Imagery:** GPU hardware, Saudi cityscapes, Arabic script rendered beautifully
- **Avoid:** Generic stock office photos, Western-only imagery

4. Proof (25-45s): Numbers, quotes, benchmarks
5. CTA (45-60s): "Try free at api.dcp.sa"
